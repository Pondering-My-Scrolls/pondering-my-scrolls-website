About

Nicky Wolfe (all pronouns) is a web developer who is confident and unafraid in speaking their truth in order to create great projects. Nicky’s special interest is web accessibility and research projects.

Jenica Woitowicz (she/her) is a web developer who loves to stay up to date on the digital world. Her interests range from web accessibility to funny robot dogs. She hopes that you find Pondering My Scrolls simple and delightful!

The Pondering My Scrolls project was created while Jenica and Nicky were chatting at a diner. It is a blog for ideas that we haven’t been able to write about yet. We will write about what we think matters most in tech right now.

Nicky Wolfe

Pronouns: All

Class: Oath of Semantics Paladin

Alignment: True Neutral

In a world where the mundane reigns supreme, Nicky Wolfe is a frontend developer who isn't afraid to stand in their truth to create otherworldly results.

With a special interest in accessibility, and an innate love of pondering orbs research projects, Pondering My Scrolls was born: a resting place for trapped ideas about what matters in tech right now.

With a little bit of time and planning, the core concept of Pondering my Scrolls was formed over a shared plate of fries and killer milkshakes at a local diner.

Jenica Woitowicz

Pronouns: she/her

Class: Alt Text Artificer

Alignment: Lawful Good

When a high intelligence modifier is paired with hard work, you get Jenica Woitowicz.

Also a frontend developer, Jenica is someone who loves to keep up to date on digital subjects. That could be anything from cutting-edge accessibility to how wonky the next dog robot looks wonky.

Jenica's strategy for making Pondering My Scrolls was to keep all the early planning notes in an ancient mutilated notebook, and to kill her darlings as much as needed to make a straightforward product that can offer some delight. And if you're reading this now, then that strategy probably paid off!
