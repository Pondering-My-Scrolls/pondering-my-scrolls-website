Accessibility

We care about accessibility!

We are happy to fix any issues, or, add new features. Please email Nicky at hello@nicky.dev.

This website was made with accessibility as a number one goal. We aim to comply with WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) version 2.2 level AA. It also complies with most of the level AAA guidelines.

We are aware of a few things that make access harder.

Known issues:

  • Our title font is not very easy to read. Individual font needs vary greatly. Our website supports plugins and browser settings that change fonts.
  • We sometimes use words that are hard to understand, for example on the homepage. These words do not have their meanings explained on the page. Pages with important content, including all articles, have simple versions.
